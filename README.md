# OmniSolve AI - Video Captioning & Distribution SaaS

OmniSolve AI is an enterprise-grade SaaS platform that automates video caption generation and multi-platform distribution. Download videos from YouTube and other sources, generate AI captions with Faster-Whisper, render platform-specific videos with FFmpeg, and publish to YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok simultaneously.

**Launch Target:** May 21, 2026 | **Status:** Development
